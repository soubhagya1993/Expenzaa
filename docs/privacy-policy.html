Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2, 2026 Last updated: April 2, 2026 App version: 1.0.0

Overview

Expenzaa ("the App", "we", "our") is a personal expense-tracking application built for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Short version: All data you enter stays on your device. Expenzaa does not have a server, does not create user accounts, and never transmits your personal or financial information anywhere.

Information We Collect

The App stores only the data you explicitly enter:

  • Expense amount — the monetary value you enter
  • Category — the category you select (e.g. Food, Transport)
  • Date — the date of the expense
  • Note — an optional description you choose to add
  • Onboarding status — a flag recording whether you have completed the intro screens

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage — the standard on-device storage mechanism provided by React Native. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any Expenzaa server (it may be included in your device's own iCloud or Google backup, which is governed by Apple's or Google's privacy policies)
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App

Third-Party Services

Expenzaa does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or data-processing services. No SDKs that transmit data externally are included in the App.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Those platforms may collect certain usage data governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

App Permissions

Expenzaa requests no system permissions beyond those required by the operating system for standard app operation. Specifically, the App does not request access to:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or media library
  • Calendar events
  • Notifications

Children's Privacy

Expenzaa is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted by the App, there is no meaningful risk to children's privacy from using Expenzaa.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are in complete control:

  • Access: Open the App at any time to view all stored data
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all data permanently
  • Portability: No account or server-side data exists to export

If your device is subject to GDPR, CCPA, or similar regulations, the above guarantees satisfy those rights because we hold no personal data about you.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes materially, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Because the App collects no personal data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.
